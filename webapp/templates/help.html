REQUEST: /teams/

RESPONSE: returns a JSON list with keys of all 30 teams
EXAMPLE:

. /teams/

. [{'team_name': Hawks},{'team_name': Celtics}...]

REQUEST: /teams/ppg/

RESPONSE: returns a JSON list with keys of all 30 teams with their points scored
EXAMPLE:

. /teams/ppg/

. [{'team_name': Hawks, 'PPG': 103},{'team_name': Celtics, 'PPG': 105}...]

REQUEST: /teams/apg/

RESPONSE: returns a JSON list with keys of all 30 teams with their assists per game
EXAMPLE:

. /teams/apg/

. [{'team_name': Hawks, 'APG': 23},{'team_name': Celtics, 'APG': 27}...]

REQUEST: /players/ppg/

RESPONSE: return a JSON list with keys of the top 10 scorers in PPG

EXAMPLE:

. /players/ppg/
. [{'Player_1': Lebron James, 'PPG1': 28, 'Player_2': Jayson Tatum, 'PPG2': 27.6...'Player_10': Kevin Durant, 'PPG10': 26.7}]

REQUEST: /players/names/

RESPONSE: return a JSON list with keys of all the NBA Players Names

EXAMPLE:

. /players/names/
. [{'Player_1': Lebron James, 'Player_2': Jayson Tatum, 'Player_1000': Kevin Durant}]

REQUEST: /games/

RESPONSE: returns a JSON list with keys of all the games and their statsitcs.

EXAMPLE

. /games/
. [{'Team': Jazz, 'Opponent': Suns, 'PTS': 95, 'PA': 106 ...}...]
